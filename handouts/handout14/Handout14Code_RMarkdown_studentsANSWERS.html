1(f)

Change coding to -1=F, 1=M (sum coding)
contr.treatment is the usual dummy (0,1) coding.
We compare the intercepts of each level to the intercept of the reference level.
In sum coding, we compare intercepts of each level to the grand mean of the intercepts.

contrasts(HHFData$Sex)=c(-1,1)
contrasts(HHFData$Sex)
##   [,1]
## F   -1
## M    1
